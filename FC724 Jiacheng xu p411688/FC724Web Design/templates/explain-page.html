Why we collect student information and feedback ?

The Reasons for Data Collection

Transparency:

  • Being clear about why data is collected helps to build trust with students.
    When students understand the reasons behind data collection,
    they may be more willing to participate and provide accurate information.

    • Educational Improvement:

  • It informs students that the data collected is used to monitor and enhance
    their learning journey, which can lead to better academic outcomes.

    • Benefits and Purposes of Data Collection

    Student Engagement:

  • collected data will be used to improve their academic and overall experience
    they're more likely to engage with the data collection process positively.

    • Continuous Improvement:

  • the benefits shows that the institution is committed to using the
    feedback to make tangible improvements in the curriculum,
    teaching methods, campus services, etc.
